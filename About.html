My Favorite Games


Baldur's Gate 3: A crpg based on Dungeons and Dragons that takes you on an adventure across Faerun, where you must deal with the Cult of the Absolute and the mind flayer threat!


Persona 5: Royal: After being wrongfully accused of a crime, you move to Tokyo and transfer to a new high school, awakening to powers that can allow you to right the wrongs of the world, but remember to keep your grades up!


Kingdom Come: Deliverance: Play as Henry, a peasant and blacksmith's son in the Kingdom of Bohemia of the Holy Roman Empire. Strive for knighthood as the feud between King Wencelas and King Sigismund brings trouble right to your doorstep.


My Favorite Books


The Illuminae Files: Follow two survivors of an attack on a faraway space colony as they uncover a conspiracy that could unravel the galaxy's largest corporation.


Assassin's Apprentice: The first book of the Farseer trilogy: Follow Fitz, the bastard son of the late Prince Chivalry, as he is introduced to his heritage, the intricacies of noble politics, and the power coursing through his bloodline.


Wotakoi: Love Is Hard for Otaku: Narumi Momose is hired as an average employee to a decent company, and she is determined to stay as normal as possible. This is immediately ruined when she runs into Hirotaka Nifuji, her childhood friend and avid gamer and otaku, who immediately out her as one of his kind.
